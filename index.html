Why us?

We offer 72 expeditions, treks and wilderness journeys to the Himalaya, Antarctica, Arctic, South America, and the Seven Summits in addition to world class mountaineering guiding services and climbing schools in New Zealand and Europe with our IFMGA / NZMGA mountain guides.
Whether you are taking your first steps into the realm of the outdoors, or are a seasoned adventurer, we believe you will find the quality of the experience offered on an Adventure Consultants expedition second to none, and we invite you to contact us to discuss your next adventure.

Trek

BOOK NOW! The stark and barren interior of Mustang is an area of Nepal that is rarely visited and tourist numbers are strictly limited each year to help preserve its uniqueness. On our trek to this Forbidden Kingdom, you can walk alongside or saddle up and ride on trusty steeds through to Lo Monthang, the hub of this tiny Kingdom. Recent trekkers have commented that the trip 'exceeded expectations', so enquire now for your opportunity to experience the history and remnants of the true Tibetan lifestyle for yourself.
